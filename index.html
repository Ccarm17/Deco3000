Undulate

Undulate is an interactive exhibit designed to allow participants to ‘take the emotional pulse of UQ.’ The rolling structure listens to mentions of UQ related hashtags on Twitter, and creates light and sound in response. Through sound users can hear the average sentiment expressed recently, and the lights each represent an individual sentiment. As sentiments come in through Twitter they are propagated through the structure. This combination of time, light and sound shows the speed of the Twitter conversation, while expressing it in a novel and directly emotional way. The use of social media allows users to directly interface with the installation, as well as to do so incidentally.
